About Us

Elite Healthcare Consulting is led by Omar Al Hajjaj, a results‑driven healthcare executive with over 23 years of experience in Saudi Arabia and the GCC. He specializes in healthcare operations, facility management and performance optimization. Omar’s expertise lies in leading large‑scale healthcare organizations, streamlining operational processes and enhancing service delivery to improve patient outcomes and financial sustainability. He has successfully managed day‑to‑day healthcare operations, optimized resource allocation and implemented efficiency‑driven solutions that drive profitability and ensure high‑quality patient care. His leadership has played a key role in turnaround strategies, mergers, acquisitions and IPO preparations, transforming healthcare facilities into high‑performing, patient‑centric organizations. As a CEO and COO in leading healthcare institutions, Omar has led initiatives to improve operational workflows, enhance supply chain management and integrate revenue cycle strategies. His experience spans facility expansion, private healthcare transformation and optimizing service delivery across hospitals, clinics and specialized medical centers. He holds an MSc in Healthcare Management and an MBA from the Swiss Business School, along with professional certifications in executive leadership, strategic planning and healthcare quality management. His focus remains on delivering operational excellence, ensuring sustainable growth and elevating healthcare standards across the region.
