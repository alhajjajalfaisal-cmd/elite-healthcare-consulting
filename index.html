About Us

Emkanat HealthCare Consulting is led by a results-driven healthcare executive with over 23 years of experience in Saudi Arabia and the GCC. He specializes in healthcare operations, facility management and performance optimization, and has a proven track record of transforming healthcare facilities into high-performing organizations. His leadership spans CEO and COO roles, guiding turnaround strategies, mergers, acquisitions, and IPO preparations. He holds an MSc in Healthcare Management and an MBA from the Swiss Business School, with professional certifications in executive leadership, strategic planning, and healthcare quality management. Emkanat HealthCare Consulting is committed to delivering operational excellence, ensuring sustainable growth and elevating healthcare standards in the region.
